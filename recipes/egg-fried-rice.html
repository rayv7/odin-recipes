Delicious egg fried rice recipe.

Egg fried rice is a quick, flavorful stir-fried rice dish made by tossing cooked rice with beaten eggs,
vegetables, and seasonings. It's versatile, budget-friendly, and an excellent way to use leftover rice.
You can serve it on its own or pair it with other dishes.

Ingredients

  • cooked rice
  • eggs
  • vegetable oil
  • chopped onions
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat a wok or large frying pan with oil until hot.
  2. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt, then scramble them lightly in the pan and set aside.
  3. Add more oil, saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant, then stir-fry vegetables until tender.
  4. Add the cold rice, breaking up clumps, and stir well to coat with oil.
  5. Return scrambled eggs to the pan, pour in soy sauce, season with salt and pepper,
    and toss everything together over high heat.
  6. Finish with spring onions or a drizzle of sesame oil, then serve hot.
